The TCP sim ports use the range starting at 9000..9100 TCP
They will be auto assigned to the simulators

For the UDP region ports you can choose your own range or just 9000..9100 UDP
The region ports don't get assigned automatically (at this time)

LSL Scripting:
For llRequestURL port 12046 TCP needs to be open
For llRequestSecureURL port 12043 TCP needs to be open
